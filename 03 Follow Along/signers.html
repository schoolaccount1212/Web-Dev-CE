Famous Signers

some of the most famous signers of the Declaration of Independence (1776) include:

Thomas Jefferson
Principal author of the Declaration of Independence
John Hancock
President of the Continental Congress, famous for his large, bold signature
Benjamin Franklin
A key diplomat and statesman
John Adams
Advocate for independence and future president
Samuel Adams
Influential revolutionary leader
